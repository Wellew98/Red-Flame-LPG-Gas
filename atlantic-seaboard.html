Why the Atlantic Seaboard Uses Red Flame

Sea Point to Bakoven, Gas Delivered to the Door.

The Atlantic Seaboard runs on gas — from apartment kitchens in Sea Point and Mouille Point to hillside homes in Bantry Bay, Clifton and Fresnaye, restaurants along the Camps Bay strip and guesthouses above Bakoven. Our Woodstock shop covers the whole strip same-day, free, with LPG refills from 5 kg and gas installations signed off with a Gas Certificate of Compliance (COC) to SANS 10087.

Sea Point and Mouille Point Apartment Gas

Beach Road, Main Road, Regent Road: buzzer access, lifts, tight parking. Our driver knows the blocks. 5 kg and 9 kg for small flats, 19 kg for larger apartments, swapped at the door on any mainstream LPG brand.

Camps Bay and Bakoven Restaurants

The Camps Bay restaurant strip and Bakoven guesthouses run on 19 kg and 48 kg cylinders. Weekly standing orders and contract pricing for commercial accounts — WhatsApp for a quote.

Gas Installation with COC Certification

New hob, geyser or pool heater for a Bantry Bay, Clifton, Fresnaye or Green Point home? We install to SANS 10087, pressure test, and issue the Gas Certificate of Compliance (COC) on the job — the document estate agents and insurers require when a property changes hands.

Gas Cages and LPG Products

Gas cages for outdoor cylinder storage at apartment blocks, restaurants and guesthouses across the Atlantic Seaboard — ventilated, lockable, compliant. Plus regulators, hoses, fittings, gas detectors and all general LPG products from our Woodstock shop.
