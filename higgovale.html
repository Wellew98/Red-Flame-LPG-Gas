Why Higgovale residents use Red Flame

Gas, up the hill, on the same day.

Higgovale is older, steeper, and greener than the rest of the City Bowl. Many homes here were built before natural gas was even an option — so LPG does the cooking, the water heating, and sometimes the fireplace. Running empty means somebody has to drive down the hill. We'd rather you didn't.

Steep-street deliveries

From Tafelberg Road down through Hof Street and Hatfield, the access roads are narrow and graded. Our van and driver handle the climb — and we carry the cylinder from the gate to wherever you keep it.

Old-home installations

If you're upgrading the gas line in a 1920s Higgovale house, we install to modern SANS 10087 standards and issue the Certificate of Compliance on the same visit. Estate agents and insurers require it at sale or renewal.

9, 19 and 48 kg — all in stock

Small flats around De Smit and Hatfield typically run 9 kg; family homes around Tafelberg Road run 19 kg; guesthouses use 48 kg banks. All sizes on the van.
