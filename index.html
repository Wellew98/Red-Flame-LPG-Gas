Frequently asked

Questions Cape Town customers ask.

Is gas delivery really free?
Yes — delivery is free within our coverage area across the City Bowl, Southern Suburbs and surrounds from our Woodstock shop. No minimum order on refills, no hidden fees.
How fast can you deliver a cylinder?
Same-day is standard if you order before mid-afternoon. WhatsApp us your address and cylinder size and we send a live ETA.
Which cylinder sizes do you stock?
9 kg (small flat, weekend braai), 19 kg (family home, full kitchen), and 48 kg (commercial kitchens, guesthouses, laundromats). All available for delivery or collection at the shop.
Can I exchange a cylinder that isn't Red Flame branded?
Yes. We exchange cylinders from any mainstream LPG brand. Bring the empty and swap for a full one at the shop, or let us do it at your door on a delivery run.
Do I need a Certificate of Compliance (COC) for my gas installation?
Yes. Under the South African Pressure Equipment Regulations, a gas COC is required for every fixed LPG installation and is mandatory when a property changes hands. We issue the COC as part of every installation we complete.
Do you do commercial gas installations for restaurants and guesthouses?
Yes — commercial installations are a core part of what we do. We design, install the cylinder bank and regulators, test, and issue the COC. Monthly contract refill pricing is available for commercial accounts.
