Frequently Asked

Questions Cape Town Customers Ask.

Is gas delivery really free?
Yes. Delivery is free from our Woodstock shop to Higgovale, Pinelands, Rondebosch, Atlantic Seaboard and the wider Cape Town City Bowl. No minimum order on refills, no hidden fees. Outside our named suburbs, WhatsApp us — we usually still deliver.
How fast can you deliver a cylinder?
Same-day is standard if you order before mid-afternoon. WhatsApp your address and cylinder size and we send a live ETA.
Which cylinder sizes do you stock?
From 5 kg upwards. 5 kg and 9 kg for small flats and braais, 14 kg and 19 kg for family kitchens, 48 kg for restaurants, guesthouses and laundromats. All available for free same-day delivery across Cape Town or collection at the shop.
How much does a gas refill cost in Cape Town?
Published refill prices at Red Flame Gas Supplier: 5 kg R175, 9 kg R350, 14 kg R515, 19 kg R700, 48 kg R1600. Delivery is free across Woodstock, Higgovale, Pinelands, Rondebosch, Atlantic Seaboard and the wider Cape Town City Bowl. South Africa's national maximum LPG price is reviewed monthly by NERSA, so we confirm the current price on WhatsApp with every order.
Can I exchange a cylinder that isn't Red Flame branded?
Yes. We exchange cylinders from any mainstream LPG brand. Bring the empty and swap for a full one at the shop, or we do it at your door on a delivery run.
Do you supply and fit gas cages?
Yes. We supply and fit outdoor gas cages for homes, restaurants and guesthouses — ventilated, lockable and compliant with SANS 10087 for safe cylinder storage.
Do you do commercial gas installations for restaurants and guesthouses?
Yes. We design, install the cylinder bank and regulators, pressure test, and sign the job off with a Gas Certificate of Compliance. Monthly contract refill pricing is available for commercial accounts.
Do you issue a gas Certificate of Compliance (COC)?
Yes. A gas COC is legally required under SANS 10087 when a property changes hands in South Africa. We issue the Certificate of Compliance on every installation, so insurers, estate agents and new owners have the document they need.
Where is your gas shop?
170 Victoria Road, Woodstock, Cape Town, 7915. Walk-in welcome during business hours, Monday to Saturday.
