Compliant work

Every install gets a real plaque and a real COC.

Below is a commercial 4-cylinder LPG bank we installed — regulator, high-pressure piping, manifold and the installer's compliance plaque on the wall. The same standard applies to a single 9 kg in a kitchen.

Our installations include:

  • Design and layout to SANS 10087-1 / 10087-7
  • Proper regulator, high-pressure line and pigtails
  • Correct ventilation and separation distances
  • Owner / Installer / SAQCC plaque fixed on-site
  • Leak test, pressure test and sign-off
  • Certificate of Compliance issued on completion

Frequently asked

Questions Cape Town customers ask us.

Do I need a Certificate of Compliance (COC) for my gas installation?
Yes. Under the Pressure Equipment Regulations (Occupational Health & Safety Act), a gas Certificate of Conformity is required for every fixed LPG installation in South Africa, and is mandatory when a property changes hands. We issue the COC as part of every installation we complete.
Can you exchange a cylinder that isn't Red Flame branded?
Yes. We exchange cylinders from any of the mainstream LPG brands — just bring the empty and swap it for a full one at the yard, or let us do it at your door on a delivery run.
How fast can you refill or swap a cylinder?
For our immediate service area (Elsies River, Ravensmead, Parow, Goodwood, Bellville, Matroosfontein, Uitsig, Elnor), same-day delivery is usually possible if you order before midday. WhatsApp us for a live ETA.
Do you do commercial gas installations for restaurants, guesthouses and laundromats?
Yes — commercial installations are a core part of what we do. We design, fabricate the cage, install the cylinder bank and regulators, test, and issue the COC. We also offer monthly contract refill pricing for commercial accounts.
Can you build a custom gas cage for an unusual space?
Yes. We have an on-site metal fabrication workshop and build gas cages in any size you need, in steel, aluminium or iron. Send us the space dimensions on WhatsApp and we will quote.
Where exactly are you based?
Unit 2, Coleman Business Park, Coleman Street, Elsies River Industrial, Cape Town, Western Cape. Walk-ins welcome during business hours.
